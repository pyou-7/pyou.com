Intro

Hello! I'm Jialin You, an Analytics Engineer with a passion for transforming raw data into meaningful insights. Currently, I'm pursuing a Master of Science in Business Analytics at UCLA Anderson, where I've developed a strong foundation in machine learning, data modeling, and statistical analysis. My technical skills include Python, SQL, and Java. I'm proficient in cloud computing platforms such as AWS, Azure, and GCP, and have experience with database storage solutions like Databricks and Snowflake. Throughout various internships and projects, I have optimized data processing and analysis workflows using both modern ETL and ELT data engineering solutions.

My professional experience includes internships at Data-Sleek and Global Music Rights as a Data Engineer and Business Intelligence Engineer respectively, where I have successfully built and maintained ETL pipelines, automated data extraction, and developed interactive dashboards in both Tableau and Power BI. I have worked extensively with tools such as Apache Spark, Airflow, and Azure Data Factory to enhance data integration processes. I am committed to leveraging my skills to solve complex data challenges and contribute to data-driven decision-making.
